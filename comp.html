TIPS

  • Listen Daily: Immerse yourself in podcasts, songs, or shows in the language to improve your understanding of natural speech.
  • Focus on Context: Even if you don’t understand every word, use context clues like tone, body language, or visuals to understand the meaning.
  • Reflection: Pause and reflect on what you’ve read or heard by summarizing key points in your own words.
  • Mix Media: Alternate between videos, audio-only, and written materials to challenge your comprehension in different ways.
