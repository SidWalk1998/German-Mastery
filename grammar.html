TIPS

  • Start With Basics: Focus on foundational grammar rules like verb conjugations and sentence structure before tackling complex topics.

  • Correct Mistakes Actively: Review your writing or speaking errors, and rewrite or restate them correctly to learn from them (going back and correcting your own work will also show you the progress you have made).
