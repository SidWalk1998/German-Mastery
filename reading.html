TIPS

Always have a note book at hand when reading. Write down repetative words/sentences that you don't regognize/know the meaning of. However, don't stop everytime you find a word to write down, highlight it and finsish the paragraph/story/article and then take notes.

  • Start Small: Begin with simple texts like children’s books or short articles to build confidence.
  • Context Clues: Focus on understanding the gist instead of every word.
  • Prompts: Write responses to prompts like "Describe your favorite meal" or "What would you do with a million dollars?"
  • Read Aloud: Practice constructing sentences with essential grammar structures.
  • Annotate: Highlight new words and phrases to revisit later.
  • Reread: Go over the same material multiple times until the meaning becomes clear. Each reread deepens your understanding and reinforces vocabulary.
  • Mix Formats: Alternate between books, articles, and short story, etc. to expose yourself to varied vocabulary.
