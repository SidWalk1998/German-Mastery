TIPS

  • Talk to Yourself: Narrate your day or describe your surroundings in the language. It’s great practice and builds confidence.
  • Shadow Native Speakers: Listen to native audio and repeat what you hear, matching tone, pace, and pronunciation.
  • Join Conversations: Practice with language partners or tutors, even if you make mistakes—it’s the fastest way to improve.
  • Use Voice Recordings: Record yourself speaking and listen back to identify areas for improvement.
