TIPS

  • Flashcards: review words with spaced repetition for better retention (quizlet is a good app to use).
  • Label Your Surroundings: Place sticky notes on everyday objects to associate their names with the items directly.
  • Use New Words Immediately: Incorporate new vocabulary into sentences or conversations as soon as you learn them.
  • Group by Themes: Learn words by category (e.g., food, travel, emotions) to make connections and improve recall.
  • Learn in Context: Read or listen to content that uses the words naturally, so you understand how they fit in real sentences.
  • Word Associations: Link new words with images, synonyms, or memorable stories to make them stick.
